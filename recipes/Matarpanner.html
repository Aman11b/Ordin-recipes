Matar Paneer

Description

Matar Paneer is a vegetarian dish made with green peas and paneer in a spiced gravy.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer (cubed)
  • 1 cup green peas
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • Tomatoes (pureed)
  • Spices (cumin, garam masala, etc.)
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan; saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add tomato puree and spices; cook until thick.
  3. Add peas and paneer; cook for a few minutes.
  4. Serve hot with naan or rice.
