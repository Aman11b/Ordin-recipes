Recipe for Chai

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups water
  • 1 cup Milk
  • teaspoon tea leaves
  • cardamom pods
  • cinnamon stick
  • Sugar to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water with spices.
  2. Add tea leaves and steep for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in milk and simmer for 5 Minutes.
  4. Strain into cups and add sugar.
