Milk Cake

Description

Milk Cake is a delicious Indian dessert made from milk and sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 liter milk
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Nuts for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil milk and curdle it with lemon juice.
  2. Drain the curds and rinse with cold water.
  3. Cook curds with sugar until thick and set in a greased tray.
  4. Cut into pieces and garnish with nuts; serve chilled.
